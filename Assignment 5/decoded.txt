Max Verstappen, born September 30, 1997, is a Dutch Formula 1 driver widely regarded as one of the 
greatest of all time. A prodigy from a young age, he became the youngest driver to start an F1 race at 17 
and the youngest to win a Grand Prix at 18. Verstappen's aggressive driving, masterful racecraft, and 
relentless consistency have redefined modern racing. He clinched his first World Championship in 2021 in 
dramatic fashion and has since dominated the sport with unmatched pace and control. A generational talent, 
Max is not just a champion, he's the gold standard in Formula 1 greatness.
